# Pass 2 — Key References

- **Attention Is All You Need** — A. Vaswani, N. Shazeer, N. Parmar, J. Uszkoreit, L. Jones, A. N. Gomez, L. Kaiser, I. Polosukhin (2017). _The Transformer architecture that BERT is built on._
- **Deep contextualized word representations (ELMo)** — M. Peters, M. Neumann, M. Iyyer, M. Gardner, C. Clark, K. Lee, L. Zettlemoyer (2018). _ELMo is the feature-based bidirectional baseline BERT improves upon._
- **Improving Language Models by Retrieving from Trillions of Tokens (RAG)** — M. Iyyer, S. Yavuz, T. Chung, N. Goyal (2020). _Not directly cited by BERT; included for context only._
- **Semi-supervised Sequence Learning** — A. M. Dai, Q. V. Le (2015). _Early work on unsupervised pre-training + fine-tuning that BERT builds on._
- **Improving Language Understanding by Generative Pre-Training (OpenAI GPT)** — A. Radford, K. Narasimhan, T. Salimans, I. Sutskever (2018). _The unidirectional fine-tuning baseline BERT improves upon._
